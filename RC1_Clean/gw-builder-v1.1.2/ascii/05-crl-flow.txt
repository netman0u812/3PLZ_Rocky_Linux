
Revocation + CRL Enforcement Flow
================================

1) Revoke user:
   gwctl revoke-user tenantA alice@outlan.net
   - openssl ca -revoke ...
   - gen CRL
   - reload nginx

2) Enforce CRL:
   gwctl mtls-enable tenantA crl=on
   - nginx uses ssl_crl <tenant>-intermediate.crl.pem

3) Automate daily refresh:
   gwctl crl-timer-enable tenantA
   - systemd timer runs gwctl crl-refresh tenantA daily
   - reloads nginx
